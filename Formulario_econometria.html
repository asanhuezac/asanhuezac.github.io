Formulario Econometría

Modelo Univariado

\[y_i=\beta_0+\beta_1x_{1,i}+u_i\] Bajo los supuestos de regresión lineal

\[\hat{\beta_o}=\overline{y}-\hat{\beta_1} \overline{x_{1}}\] \[\hat{\beta_1}=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{T} (x_{1,i}-\overline{x_{1}})(y_i-\overline{y})}{\sum_{i=1}^{T} (x_{1,i}-\overline{x_{1}})^2}=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{T} (x_{1,i}-\overline{x_1})y_i}{\sum_{i=1}^{T} (x_{1,i}-\overline{x_1})^2}=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{T} (y_i-\overline{y})x_{1,i}}{\sum_{i=1}^{T} (x_{1,i}-\overline{x_{1}})^2}\]

\[\hat{y_i}=\hat{\beta_0}+\hat{\beta_1}x_{1.i}\] \[\hat{u_i}=y_i-\hat{y_i}\]

\[\mathbb{E} [\hat{\beta_0}]=\beta_0\] \[\mathbb{E} [\hat{\beta_1}]=\beta_1\]

\[Var(\hat{\beta_0})=\frac{\sigma^2 \sum_{i=1}^T x_{1,i}^2}{T \cdot \sum_{i=1}^T (x_{1,i}-\overline{x_1})^2}=\frac{\sigma^2 \sum_{i=1}^T x_{1,i}^2}{T \cdot SST_1}\] \[Var(\hat{\beta_1})=\frac{\sigma^2}{\sum_{i=1}^T (x_{1.i}-\overline{x_1})^2}=\frac{\sigma^2}{SST_1}\]

\[\hat{\sigma}^2=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^T \hat{u_i}^2 }{T-K-1}\]

\(R^2\)

\[SST=SSE+SSR\]

\[\sum_{i=1}^{T} (y_i-\overline{y})^2 = \sum_{i=1}^{T} (\hat{y_i}-\overline{y})^2 + \sum_{i=1}^{T} (y_i-\hat{y_i})^2\]

\[R^2=\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{T} (\hat{y_i}-\overline{y})^2}{\sum_{i=1}^{T} (y_i-\overline{y})^2}=1-\frac{\sum_{i=1}^{T} (y_i-\hat{y_i})^2}{\sum_{i=1}^{T} (y_i-\overline{y})^2}\]

Modelo Multivariado

\[y_i=\beta_0+\beta_1x_{1,i}+\beta_2x_{2,i}+...+\beta_kx_{k,i}+u_i\]

Bajo los supuestos de regresión lineal

\[\mathbb{E} [\hat{\beta_j}]=\beta_j\] \[Var(\hat{\beta_j})=\frac{\sigma^2}{SST_j(1-R_j^2)}=\frac{\sigma^2}{SST_j}\cdot VIF_j\]

Modelos Regresión \(\beta_1\) \(Pendiente=\frac{\partial y}{\partial x}\) Interpretación
Nivel-Nivel \(y_i=\beta_0+\beta_1x_i+u_i\) \(\frac{\Delta y_i}{\Delta x_i}\) \(\beta_1\) Cambio en nivel
Nivel-Log \(y_i=\beta_0+\beta_1 ln(x_i)+u_i\) \(\frac{\Delta y_i}{\Delta \%x_i}\) \(\beta_1 \frac{1}{x}\) Semi-Elasticidad
Log-Nivel \(ln(y_i)=\beta_0+\beta_1x_i+u_i\) \(\frac{\Delta \%y_i}{\Delta x_i}\) \(\beta_1 y\) Semi-Elasticidad
Log-Log \(ln(y_i)=\beta_0+\beta_1 ln(x_i)+u_i\) \(\frac{\Delta \%y_i}{\Delta \%x_i}\) \(\beta_1 \frac{y}{x}\) Elasticidad

\[\mathbb{E}[\hat{\gamma_1}]=\beta_1+\underbrace{\beta_2 \frac{Cov(X_1,X_2)}{Var(X_1)}}_{\text Sesgo}\]

\(Cov(X_1,X_2)>0\) \(Cov(X_1,X_2)<0\)

Inferencia

\[t_{\hat{\beta_j}}= \frac{\hat{\beta_j}-\beta_j}{sd(\hat{\beta_j})} \sim t_{(T-K-1)}\]

\[Pr\left[ \hat{\beta_j}-c_{\alpha} \cdot sd(\hat{\beta_j}) \leq \beta_j \leq \hat{\beta_j}+c_{\alpha} \cdot sd(\hat{\beta_j})\right]=1-\alpha\]

\[P_{value}=Pr[|t_{obs}|\geq |t_{\hat{\beta_j}}|]\]

\[F_{obs}=\frac{(SSR_{r}-SSR_{ur})/q}{(SSR_{ur})/(T-K-1)}=\frac{(R_{ur}^2-R_{r}^2)/q}{(1-R_{ur}^2)/(T-K-1)} \sim F_{q, t-k-1}\]

Modelo de Rubin \[\underbrace{E[Y_i/W_i=1]-E[Y_i/W_i=0]}_{Diferencia \hspace{1mm} Promedio \hspace{1mm} Observable} =\underbrace{E[Y_{1,i}/W_i=1]-E[Y_{0,i}/W_i=1]}_{ATT}+\underbrace{E[Y_{0,i}/W_i=1]-E[Y_{0,i}/W_i=0]}_{Sesgo \hspace{1mm} de \hspace{1mm} Selección}\] \[ATE=E[Y_1-Y_0]\] \[ATT=E[Y_1-Y_0/W_i=1]\] \[ITT=E[Y_i/Z_i=1]-E[Y_i/Z_i=0]=ATT \cdot Pr[W_i=1/Z_i=1]\]

Modelos AR(P) \[E[y_t]=\frac{\phi_0}{(1-\sum_{i=1}^{p}\phi_i)}\] \[Var[y_t]=\sigma_\varepsilon^2+\sum_{i=1}^{p}\phi_i\gamma_i\] \[Cov[y_t,y_{t-1}]=\sum_{i=1}^{P}\phi_i\gamma_{j-i} \hspace{3mm} \forall j\geq 1\] \[Corr[y_t,y_{t-s}]=\phi_1^s\]

Modelos MA(q) \[E[y_t]=\theta_0\] \[Var[y_t]=\left(1+\sum_{i=1}^{q} \theta_i^2\right) \sigma_\varepsilon^2\] \[Cov[y_t,y_{t-s}]=\begin{cases} \sigma_\varepsilon^2 \sum_{j=0}^{q-s} \theta_j \theta_{j+s} & \text{si } s\leq q \hspace{3mm} donde \hspace{2mm} \theta_0=1\\ 0 & \text{si } s> q \\ \end{cases}\]

\[Corr[y_t,y_{t-s}]=\begin{cases} \frac{\sum_{j=0}^{q-s} \theta_j \theta_{j+s}}{(1+\sum_{i=1}^{q} \theta_i^2)} & \text{si } s\leq q \hspace{3mm} donde \hspace{2mm} \theta_0=1\\ 0 & \text{si } s> q \\ \end{cases}\]
